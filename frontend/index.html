Fequently Asked Questions

You Asked and we Answered.

How do I calculate my eligible B-BBEE points through Odin Education?

You are able to use our easy-to-use and quick online B-BBEE tool to calculate your eligible points and spend targets based on your projected NPAT for your financial year end. The tool allows you to select the areas of the score card you would like to earn points in, as well as select your impact preference. It’s that simple!

How does Odin Education help me earn points on my scorecard?

Odin Education is able to assist you in earning points in three of the components of your B-BBEE scorecard; Enterprise Development, Supplier Development and Socio-economic Development. Our organisation has built an ecosystem with partners in each of these areas who contribute to the overall impact of your investment. Our partners are Polelo Unlimited (Enterprise Development), Tsogang (Supplier Development) and Omang Foundation (Socio-economic Development. Learn more about these collaborations here.

What happens after I complete the online B-BBEE assessment?

Our system will automatically generate an obligation-free, customised quotation and complete audit pack which meets your specific B-BBEE needs. All this in just 5 minutes!

What happens during the call with an Impact Consultant from Odin Education?

Our friendly Impact Consultants will call you within the next business day to discuss how you wish to make an impact using our Education Ecosystem, as well as discuss your various PR opportunities and media options for the launch event that are included in our service.

Is it possible to make an impact in my community?

Absolutely! Odin Education works with client companies to assess, deploy and maintain our education solution using technology, in the community of choice for our client. We believe that only through companies having a vested interest in the growth and development of their own communities can we truly achieve sustainable transformation for all.

Will Odin Education provide me with the necessary documentation required for my audit?

Odin Education provides you with a complete audit pack based on your requirements identified when using our B-BBEE Online Tool. Not only does this pack include all the documentation you need to successfully pass your audit, but it is sent to you within 5 minutes, never has it been this quick or this easy! How soon will I receive a quotation and my audit pack?

Within 5 minutes from your completion of the online B-BBEE Tool, we will send you an obligation-free quote, along with the draft audit pack containing the required documents as per your assessment.

How soon will I hear from you, after completing the online assessment?

If you choose to accept the quote, one of our friendly impact consultants will call you within the next business day to discuss your impact options using our Odin Education Ecosystem.

Odin Education has a passionate and skilled team of Marketing professionals who take the PR burden of showcasing your investment off your shoulders. Leave it to us and we will take care of planning the launch event (symbolic handover), press releases, media outreach, social media campaigns and marketing value measurement.

How often does Odin Education report on impact and ROI?

We are committed to providing our clients with detailed quarterly reports which highlight the impact made by their investment. The impact is measured by engagement analytics, content analysis, engagement campaigns and activities, and learner feedback.
