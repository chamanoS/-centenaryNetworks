A Flame That Never Fades.

Tech Academy

~ Data Science Certificates, short courses in IT Support, UX Design, Digital Marketing, Ecommerce & Ai.

The Foundation aims to empower 10,000 learners with computer science skills to prepare them for the 'Jobs of the Future.' Leading companies such as Google, IBM, and Microsoft offer free online courses in Data Analytics, and the Foundation is facilitating access to these transformative opportunities for communities that might otherwise miss out.


We support our communities by providing the necessary tools for accessing these opportunities, having already acquired 250 tablets and planning additional laptop procurements, recognizing that not all programs can be effectively learned on tablets alone.

Soccer Academy

~ Soccer Couching Skills & learners, girls as well as boys, soccer skills

Collaborating with the Department of Education and the University of Pretoria, we offer coaching, mentoring, and establish pathways for aspiring sports icons. Our goal extends beyond merely developing athletes; we aim to cultivate well-rounded individuals and community influencers.


We sponsor opportunities for youth coaches to attain qualifications as CAF professionals and subsequently place them in our adopted schools. Here, they engage in training our learners through the Icons Cup program and Soccer Academy. This not only benefits the learners but also provides the coaches with the work experience necessary for potential placement as assistant coaches on professional teams

You Asked and we Answered.

How do I calculate my eligible B-BBEE points through HM Pitje Foundation?

You are able to use our easy-to-use and quick online B-BBEE tool to calculate your eligible points and spend targets based on your projected NPAT for your financial year end. The tool allows you to select the areas of the score card you would like to earn points in, as well as select your impact preference. Itâ€™s that simple!

How does HM Pitje Foundation help me earn points on my scorecard?

HM Pitje Foundation is able to assist you in earning points in three of the components of your B-BBEE scorecard; Enterprise Development, Supplier Development and Socio-economic Development. Our organisation has built an ecosystem with partners in each of these areas who contribute to the overall impact of your investment. Learn more about these collaborations here.

What happens after I complete the online B-BBEE assessment?

Our system will automatically generate an obligation-free, customised quotation and complete audit pack which meets your specific B-BBEE needs. All this in just 5 minutes!

What happens during the call with an Impact Consultant from HM Pitje Foundation?

Our friendly Impact Consultants will call you within the next business day to discuss how you wish to make an impact using our Education Ecosystem, as well as discuss your various PR opportunities and media options for the launch event that are included in our service.

Is it possible to make an impact in my community?

Absolutely! HM Pitje Foundation works with client companies to assess, deploy and maintain our education solution using technology, in the community of choice for our client. We believe that only through companies having a vested interest in the growth and development of their own communities can we truly achieve sustainable transformation for all.

Will HM Pitje Foundation provide me with the necessary documentation required for my audit?

HM Pitje Foundation provides you with a complete audit pack based on your requirements identified when using our B-BBEE Online Tool. Not only does this pack include all the documentation you need to successfully pass your audit, but it is sent to you within 5 minutes, never has it been this quick or this easy! How soon will I receive a quotation and my audit pack?

Within 5 minutes from your completion of the online B-BBEE Tool, we will send you an obligation-free quote, along with the draft audit pack containing the required documents as per your assessment.

How soon will I hear from you, after completing the online assessment?

If you choose to accept the quote, one of our friendly impact consultants will call you within the next business day to discuss your impact options using our HM Pitje Foundation Ecosystem.

Who plans the PR event and handles the Marketing aspect of the investment?

HM Pitje Foundation has a passionate and skilled team of Marketing professionals who take the PR burden of showcasing your investment off your shoulders. Leave it to us and we will take care of planning the launch event (symbolic handover), press releases, media outreach, social media campaigns and marketing value measurement.

How often does HM Pitje Foundation report on impact and ROI?

We are committed to providing our clients with detailed quarterly reports which highlight the impact made by their investment. The impact is measured by engagement analytics, content analysis, engagement campaigns and activities, and learner feedback.
